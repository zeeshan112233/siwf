    Why should I care

    • A large number of people donot get anything to eat all day. Numerous deaths are attributed to undernutrition.
    • More than 23 Million Children donot go to schools. We need to work to reduce this.
    • A large number of people are homeless specially elder people suffer due to lack of homes.
    • Due to high prices in healthcare sector, many poor people cannnot go to hospitals and are suffering !
    • Mental health issues are rising day by day in our country and Awareness needs to be raised in this regard.

    How you can Help

    • We want to do more and you can help. By committing a small fraction of your income , you can help save a child and contribute to humanity. You can donate as low as PKR 500 per month.
    • While we appreciate monthly donations more where you choose to donate a small fixed amount monthly for us to be able to plan our programs for longer term, a single donation can also help save a life.
    • All our projects are transparent and you can be sure of your donations will be used for a good caouse. You will get updates on how you are making a difference.
